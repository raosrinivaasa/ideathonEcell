ABOUT IDEATHON

Ideathon is all about showcasing ideas and weighing their potential benefits to society. It is an ideal opportunity for you to provide a viable solution to society's problems or to come up with the best possible cocktail of idea and innovation. NMIT's ideathon is tailored to provide participants a first hand trial to the Entrepreneurial journey. It offers a competitive model for showcasing original thinking and problem solving aptitiude among the participants During ideathon participants from various sectors must use their innovative learning practices to ideate possible solutions for the issues people are facing.

OUR VISION

We at ECELL - NMIT believe that the only constant is change. We want to be a part of the transformation that will shape new businesses. As a result, with ideathon we aim to provide an opportunity for unique ideas, fresh approaches, and new solutions. We want our participants to rethink, redefine, and broaden the concept of entrepreneurship. There is a lot to gain from this event, including the opportunity to learn from a highly experienced jury and the chance to win cash awards worth up to Rs.40000. Thus IDEATHON is the ideal occasion to stake your claim in the booming business community
